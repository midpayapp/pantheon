FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about joining the MidPay waitlist.

How many spots are available in the beta program?

We're limiting our beta launch to the first 50 contractors who join our waitlist. This ensures we can provide personalized onboarding and support to each early adopter.

When exactly will the beta launch?

Our beta is scheduled to launch in April 2025. Exact dates will be communicated to waitlist members as we get closer to launch.

Is there a cost to join the waitlist?

No, joining the waitlist is completely free. You'll only pay the escrow fee percentage when you start using the platform for your projects.

Can I cancel my waitlist spot if I change my mind?

Yes, you can cancel your spot on the waitlist at any time by contacting us at info@midpay.ai.

Will my clients need to sign up for MidPay too?

Your clients will need to create a basic account to fund the escrow and approve milestones, but there's no cost to them. We'll provide you with materials to help explain the benefits to your clients.
