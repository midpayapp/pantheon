FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about MidPay's secure payment platform for contractors.

How does MidPay protect my payments?

MidPay uses secure escrow accounts to hold client funds before work begins. These funds are only released when agreed-upon milestones are completed and verified. This ensures you never start work without knowing the money is available and secured.

What does MidPay cost?

MidPay offers two pricing plans: a Premium Plan at $199/month with a 2% escrow fee, and a Free Plan with no monthly fee but a 5% escrow fee. The Premium Plan is ideal for contractors with regular projects, while the Free Plan works well for occasional projects or those just getting started.

How quickly do I receive payment?

Once a milestone is verified as complete, payment is released to your account within 24 hours. This is significantly faster than the industry average of 45+ days for construction payments.
