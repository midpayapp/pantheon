FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting MidPay.

What are your business hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Standard Time. Email inquiries received outside of business hours will be addressed on the next business day.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line at (201) 844-7662 during business hours for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a demo of the MidPay platform?

Yes! We offer personalized demos of the MidPay platform for contractors interested in learning more. You can request a demo through our contact form by selecting "Request a Demo" from the inquiry type dropdown, or by emailing demo@midpay.ai directly.

I'm a homeowner. Can I contact MidPay directly?

Absolutely! While MidPay is designed primarily for contractors, we're happy to answer questions from homeowners about how our platform works. Feel free to reach out through any of our contact methods, and we'll provide information on how MidPay can benefit your construction projects.

Do you offer support for technical issues?

Yes, our technical support team is available to assist with any platform-related issues. For the most efficient service, please provide detailed information about the problem you're experiencing, including any error messages and the steps that led to the issue.
