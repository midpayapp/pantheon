KEY BENEFITS FOR CONTRACTORS

MidPay delivers tangible advantages that directly impact your construction business's success and profitability.

Guaranteed Payment for Completed Work

No more wondering if you'll get paid. With funds secured in escrow before you start, payment for completed milestones is guaranteed. Eliminate the risk of non-payment entirely.

Improved Cash Flow

Receive payment as soon as milestones are verified—no more 30, 60, or 90-day waits. Keep your business running smoothly with predictable cash flow that supports growth and stability.

Reduced Payment Disputes

Clear milestone definitions and verification processes dramatically reduce misunderstandings and payment conflicts, saving you time, stress, and potential legal costs.

Enhanced Professional Image

Offering secure, transparent payment options shows clients you're a serious professional who values clarity and trust in business relationships. Stand out from competitors.

Seamless Integration

MidPay integrates with the contractor CRM platforms you already use, including Jobber, Contractor+, and Housecall Pro. Maintain your existing workflow while adding payment security.

Mobile Access

Manage projects, submit verification photos, and receive payments from anywhere with our mobile-friendly platform. Perfect for contractors who are always on the move between job sites.
