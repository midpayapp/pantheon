FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about MidPay's pricing and payment structure.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. MidPay's pricing is completely transparent. You pay either the monthly subscription fee plus the escrow fee percentage, or just the higher escrow fee percentage with the free plan. There are no setup fees, cancellation fees, or other hidden charges.

How is the escrow fee calculated?

The escrow fee is calculated as a percentage of the total project value. For example, if you're on the Premium Plan with a 2% escrow fee and your project is worth $10,000, the escrow fee would be $200.

Who pays the escrow fee?

The escrow fee is paid by the contractor (you). However, many contractors choose to build this fee into their project quotes, effectively passing the cost to the client while still offering the security and peace of mind that comes with MidPay.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade mid-month, we'll prorate the difference. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No, MidPay is offered on a month-to-month basis with no long-term commitment required. You can cancel at any time.

Do you offer discounts for annual payment?

Yes, we offer a 15% discount when you pay annually for the Premium Plan. This option will be available after our beta launch period.
