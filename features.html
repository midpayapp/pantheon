MidPay offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed specifically for contractors to ensure payment security and project success.

CORE FEATURES

Our platform is built from the ground up to address the unique payment challenges faced by construction contractors.

Secure Escrow Protection

Our platform holds funds securely in FDIC-insured escrow accounts, releasing them only when agreed-upon construction milestones are completed and verified.

  • ✓ FDIC-insured accounts for maximum security
  • ✓ Transparent fund status visible to both parties
  • ✓ Automated release upon milestone verification

Construction Milestone Tracking

MidPay allows funds to be released incrementally as specific construction phases are completed, ensuring contractors get paid promptly for work done.

  • ✓ Customizable milestone definitions
  • ✓ Photo/video verification of completed work
  • ✓ Automated payment notifications

Digital Contracts

Create clear, detailed agreements that outline project scope, timeline, milestones, and payment terms to prevent misunderstandings.

  • ✓ Construction-specific contract templates
  • ✓ Electronic signature capabilities
  • ✓ Automatic milestone schedule creation

Mobile Documentation

Capture and share project progress directly from the job site with our mobile-friendly platform.

  • ✓ Photo/video uploads from mobile devices
  • ✓ GPS-tagged verification
  • ✓ Real-time progress updates

PROACTIVE MILESTONE COMMUNICATION

We prevent disputes by verifying satisfaction at each milestone rather than waiting until project completion.

Our dedicated team calls homeowners at each milestone completion to verify work quality and satisfaction. This real-time verification process ensures issues are identified and addressed immediately, preventing end-of-project disputes and creating a documented approval history.

How It Works:

  • 1. Contractor marks milestone as complete in the platform
  • 2. Our team contacts the homeowner within 24 hours to verify satisfaction
  • 3. Any concerns are addressed immediately before payment is released
  • 4. Upon verification, payment is released within 24 hours
